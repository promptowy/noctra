Privacy Policy

Noctra Browser · Effective date: 12 June 2026
TL;DR — we collect nothing.

1. DATA CONTROLLER

Promptowy (sole proprietorship / JDG), Warsaw, Poland.

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

Noctra Browser does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely on your device.

3. DATA STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

— Bookmarks you save
— Browser settings and preferences
— Browsing profiles (names and colors)
— Sites where you have disabled the ad blocker

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. AD BLOCKING

The app uses a locally bundled domain blocklist (EasyList/EasyPrivacy) to block ads and trackers. This filtering happens entirely on your device. No request data is sent anywhere.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Noctra Browser does not integrate any analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

6. PERMISSIONS

The app requests only the INTERNET permission, required to load web pages. No access to contacts, camera, microphone, location, or files is requested.

7. CHILDREN

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.
