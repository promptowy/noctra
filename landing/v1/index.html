Why Noctra

The modern web watches you read. We think that's absurd.

An average news page makes dozens of requests to companies whose only job is to remember you were there. Browsers treat this as normal. Noctra treats it as noise — and cancels it at the network level, before a single byte arrives.

How it works

  1. Trackers blocked, not hidden

    Requests to ad and analytics servers are cancelled outright. Pages load faster because the heaviest parts never download.

  2. Cookie walls dissolved

    Consent overlays and "disable your adblocker" nags are removed automatically. You read the article, not the legal department.

  3. Profiles that never touch

    Work and private life get separate cookies, logins and storage. A colour-coded dot on every tab tells you which world you're in.

  4. Nothing phones home

    Noctra has no telemetry, no accounts, no news feed, no token economy. It is a browser. That used to be normal too.
